The Green Mile

The Green Mile is a deeply emotional and supernatural drama by Stephen King, set in a 1930s Southern prison. It follows Paul Edgecombe, a death row supervisor, whose life is forever changed by John Coffey, a towering yet gentle inmate with mysterious healing powers. As Paul uncovers the truth behind Coffey’s conviction, the story explores themes of justice, compassion, and the burden of miracles.

It’s a tale that blends gritty realism with magical elements, all wrapped in a haunting reflection on humanity
